Baohua Yang is currently a senior researcher at IBM. His interests mainly include key techniques of Cloud Computing, Fintech, analytics and optimization, especially on emerging technologies (e.g., SDN, NFV, BigData, container, IoT and Blockchain). He has published several books and 10s of papers, and submitted nearly 10 patents in those areas.

He has lead the architecture design and system implementation of enterprise solutions, and solved key technical problems for customer productions.

As an active contributor, he has submitted code, proposals, and presentations to many Open-Source projects (e.g., OpenStack, OpenvSwitch, Docker, OpenDaylight, Mesos, Kubernetes, Hyperledger), and has lead several Open-Source projects including easyOVS and Hyperledger-py.

He has published several articles in top-quality international conference and journals (e.g., IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE Trans on Computers), and several technical books. He is currently the TPC member/reviewer of numbers of conferences and journals.

Curriculum vitae is available: English and Chinese. You can also find him at Linkedin.
