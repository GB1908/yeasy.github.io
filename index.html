Baohua Yang received the B.S. degree from the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in the year 2007 . He continues with his Ph.D. and master's degrees since then, under the supervision of Dr. Jun Li at Tsinghua University.

        Currently, he is a Ph.D Candidate in the NSLab at RIIT of Tsinghua University, an IEEE & IEEE Communications Society Member, and TPC member/reviewer for several conferences and journals. 

        Baohua Yang's research interests mainly include algorithmic optimization, security and performance issues in computer networking and architectures, specially on data center architecture and future network management technologies. His recent projects are based on SDN (OpenFlow, OpenvSwitch, etc.) and CCN. More infomation could be found at the Research page.

        He has joined IBM Research after graduation, and his Ph.D thesis is available here.
